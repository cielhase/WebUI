Animal Rescue Foundation (ARF)

"Helping dogs & cats find forever homes."

HELLO,

Whether you want to adopt a new loved one or help out and volunteer at this rescue shelter, we're glad to have you join us. Located in Beacon of New York, we take in the furry canines and felines that don't have a home (yet) and make sure they are properly cared for. This place is a no-kill shelter, but our most pressing goal is to place every one of these lovable pets into a warm, forever home.

"Welcome" from Milky, one of our rescue dogs!

These animals are still waiting to find a forever home. Whether it's puppies, kittens, senior dogs, or special-needs cats you're looking to care for, we're positive we can find a new furry family member for you.
Check out our dogs and cats for adoption!

Every donation counts-- please consider donating to ARF, no matter how small! All donations go into maintaining housing for the dogs and cats, in addition to food and medicine. All donations are appreciated.

Thank you.
